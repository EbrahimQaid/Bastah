Act as a senior UX/UI designer and full-stack product expert.

Your task: design a luxurious, mobile-first multi-vendor e-commerce platform tailored for small sellers who sell primarily via WhatsApp.

The platform must have:

1. **Customer Experience (UX/UI)**:
- Mobile-first, clean, elegant, and premium look
- High-quality product grid and product cards
- Smooth micro-interactions for Add to Cart, Hover, Clicks, and Page Transitions
- Sticky cart with mini-cart popup after adding an item
- Seamless flow: Add to Cart → Mini Cart → Checkout → Order Confirmation
- Checkout:
  - Fields: Name, Phone, Address, Notes
  - Minimal steps, distraction-free
  - "Place Order" button
  - WhatsApp integration invisible to customer
- Multi-language support: Arabic (RTL) and English (LTR) with toggle
- Theme customization: primary color, secondary color, background color; all UI elements adapt automatically
- Smart filters and search:
  - Price range, Category, Size, Color
  - Live search with suggestions
- Product page:
  - Multiple images (carousel + zoom)
  - Variant selection (Size, Color)
  - Stock indicator ("Only few left")
  - Ratings and reviews

2. **Seller Dashboard**:
- Extremely simple, mobile-friendly, fast
- Add/edit/delete products quickly (<30 sec)
- Upload multiple images (Drag & Drop)
- Add product variants (size, color)
- Add external link per product
- Category management (add, edit, reorder)
- Orders:
  - List orders with status (New, Contacted, Completed)
  - Open order in WhatsApp button (backend)
- Store customization:
  - Store name, banner image, theme colors
- Preview product immediately after adding

3. **Design System / UI Details**:
- Minimal, premium, and uncluttered
- Primary color: #C1121F
- Background: #FFFFFF
- Surface: #F3F4F6
- Text primary: #1F2937
- Text secondary: #6B7280
- Rounded corners, soft shadows
- Elegant typography (Poppins/Inter)
- Smooth transitions, micro-interactions
- Plenty of whitespace
- Fast loading, optimized for low internet speed

4. **Goal**:
- Deliver a luxurious, professional, and highly intuitive shopping experience
- Make customers feel trust and sophistication
- Make small sellers enjoy fast, simple, and rewarding dashboard
- All WhatsApp interactions hidden from the customer, seamless behind the scenes

Output: a full visual and UX design, component layout, interaction behavior, and page flow for both Customer and Seller Dashboard.
